What is PT Ready?

PT Ready is an unofficial PFRA fitness preparation tool built for U.S. Air Force members. It uses the March 2026 PFRA scoring standards and the Warfighter's Fitness Playbook 2.0 (Feb 2026) to help you calculate your score, generate personalized AI-driven training plans, and track your progress — all in one place on your phone.

Featurestap to expand▼
  • Score Calculator — Enter your push-ups, sit-ups, cardio, and waist measurement to get your total PFRA score and component breakdown by age group and gender. Includes built-in event timers and official HAMR audio to facilitate a mock test.
  • AI Training Plan — Generate a personalized training plan based on your scores and selected test date. Plans follow the Warfighter's Fitness Playbook structure with warm-up, main workout, and cool-down.
  • Workout Timers — Tap any rest or hold to launch a fullscreen countdown timer that auto-returns when done.
  • Workout Log — Log daily sessions and track completion across your plan.
  • Score History — Save and review past scores to see your progress over time.
  • PDF Export — Download your training plan as a formatted PDF.
How to Usetap to expand▼
  • Start on the Calculator tab — enter your age, gender, and scores, then tap Calculate.
  • Go to AI Plan — review your scores and tap Generate Plan. This calls the AI to build a custom program length based on your selected test date.
  • Navigate days with the arrows. Tap exercises to check them off. Tap rest buttons to start a timer.
  • Tap Log This Workout when done to record the session.
  • Use the History tab to review past scores and plan progress.
